Lead & communications automation platform

For a Canadian real-estate and property-management firm, I built a multi-source lead engine that runs on its own every day: browser-based scrapers across several listing platforms, an AI layer that classifies each lead by intent, and an automated handoff into the CRM with phone validation and cross-source deduplication.

  • Browser scrapers with anti-bot handling across multiple sources
  • AI + rule-based classification to qualify every lead
  • Clean CRM sync with strict dedup and validation
  • Call/SMS analysis that surfaces missed opportunities
  • Compliance-aware, read-only enrichment — no surprise outbound
